Career

We are a startup on a schedule to deliver our MVP (minimal viable product) which includes a decentralized marketplace, encrypted communication tools, a built in confidential currency and integrations with other crypto-currency projects to provide a use-case for any coin.

The marketplace and underlying privacy platform will feature confidential transactions, 2-party escrow, decentralized self-governance and open doors for person to person global online shopping.

We are actively seeking passionate, driven creatives in the following career paths:
