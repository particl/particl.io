Particl Desktop (with Open Marketplace)

Particl Desktop is Particl’s main GUI wallet. It’s beautiful and feature-rich client, recommended for most users.

To enable Open Marketplace, you need to create a new wallet named Market.

Particl Core (Qt) (with Ledger wallet support)

Particl Qt is suitable for everyone and highly recommended for beginners, as it's feature-rich, user-friendly and easy to setup. Download latest release, double-click Particl executable (in bin/ directory) and you're done.
