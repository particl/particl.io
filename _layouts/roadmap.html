Particl Project
Roadmap

For more details on the Particl project’s development status, visit our Status Report page.

Updated 28 August 2018
In Progress Testnet/Mainnet

Market Listing Governance

The Marketplace Governance system will allow users to self-moderate the content listed on Particl Marketplace. This system is entirely controlled by the community and no Particl team member or staff has any authority over the content of the marketplace.

In Progress Testnet/Mainnet

Multiwallets

Multiwallets will allow you to set up multiple wallets within the same Particl Desktop client, dramatically increasing your security, privacy, and usability by isolating your data and history used on different profiles.

In Progress Testnet/Mainnet

Particl Marketplace (beta)

The Beta version of Particl Marketplace will introduce several new critical features to its mainnet deployment. Among these changes, users can expect to see the Marketplace Governance mechanism as well as the RingCT enhancement made to our current MAD escrow system. On top of that, other less critical features might be included as well, including private listings and markets, deposit negotiations, multiwallets, settings page, and more.

In Progress Mainnet

Software Development Kit (SDK)

Particl’s SDK will provide any developer with the required tools and environment to easily start contributing to the Particl network by either creating their own decentralized applications or improving already-existing products such as Particl Marketplace or Particl Desktop by adding to them new features.

In Progress

Community Fund Vote

There is currently a leftover from the Community Initiative of ~33,000 PART which is entirely owned by the community. As these funds are simply held by the Particl Foundation and not owned by it, a consensus among the community must be reached in order to move or utilize those funds. Proposal voting rounds will be initiated as soon as the Project Governance mechanism hits mainnet!

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

iOS Copay mobile wallet

Particl’s mobile wallet, Particl Copay, is currently only available for Android devices as well as desktops. We plan on releasing an iOS version for it to be published on Apple’s App Store.

MP Reputation

How do you protect against sybil attacks and gaming of reputation in a privacy focused marketplace?

This is a big question that requires some serious R&D. Everything is 10× harder when applying privacy to it. Reputation is important in ecommerce. The development and research teams will spend a lot of time flushing out the importance and risks of reputation in a privacy focused marketplace.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

ZMQ for MP Messages

Short for ZeroMQ, this is an automated messaging system that allows messages and notifications to be automatically pushed to Particl Desktop. An example would be if Particl Desktop has an update available, ZMQ will push notification to Particl Desktop and users will see a notice a new version of the Desktop is available.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

Particl Marketplace Release

All versions of the MP leading up to full release will be testnet versions with new features introduced with each release. Particl Marketplace’s mainnet requirements are as follow: a working decentralized Marketplace Governance mechanism in place and a RingCT-enhanced escrow system, both on mainnet. The first mainnet iteration of Particl Marketplace may also contain other features such currency-agnosticism (accepting multiple coins through atomic swaps or third-party integrations), multiwallets, private listings and markets, deposit negotiations, and etc.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

Bulletproofs: Making Privacy Transactions Scalable

Bulletproofs are short non-interactive zero-knowledge proofs that require no trusted setup. They are designed to enable efficient Confidential Transactions (CT), and thus RingCT, effectively reducing their transaction size by about ~80%. We estimate the research, development, implementation, and testing to take around 3 to 4 months as a part-time assignment for Tecnovert, our Lead Core Developer & Cryptographer.

Planned Mainnet

RingCT Hardfork

Particl’s Bitcoin-based implementation of the RingCT privacy protocol has been on testnet since Particl’s inception but is planned on being moved over to mainnet soon in combination with the Marketplace Governance mechanism. Particl’s own custom implementation of RingCT has been properly audited by WSA and academically reviewed by the NJIT Department of Technology and no major issue was found by neither inspections.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

Copay Cold Staking

Particl Copay is a secure but simple wallet made for desktop and mobile OS. We plan on integrating cold staking into it, thus allowing users to add multi-signature security parameters to their cold staking or hardware cold staking setup.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

Lightning Network

The Lightning Network is a decentralized network built using smart-contract functionality to enable instant, near-zero fee payments across a network of participants. It also enables truly decentralized and efficient atomic swaps to take place as well as help the blockchain support thousands of transactions per second. This is a big and high-priority item on our roadmap as we build products able to sustain heavy traffic as well as paths for other cryptocurrencies to be used on Particl Marketplace without having to rely on any third-party.

Planned Testnet/Mainnet

Blind Lightning

Bling Lightning is our very own projected custom implementation of the Lightning Network with added support for CT and RingCT privacy transactions. This would allow for anonymous atomic swaps as well as improved privacy within the Lightning Network itself.

Completed Testnet/Mainnet
